How to play

Prepare "empty" souls for their next life by giving them traits: good vs. bad, optimism vs. pessimism, romantism vs. perversity

1. Create a new "empty" soul by using the pump.

2. Place the soul in front a TV for a sufficient time, to give it the expected caracteristics.

3. Wait for angels or succubus to bring tokens that power the TV with specific programs.

4. Shoot angels/succubus that bring undesired tokens.

5. Deliver the soul to the exit when it is ready!

The souls to build are displayed in the top-left corner. Caracteristics can be assigned in any order. Souls can be built in any order. Just take care of the time left to accomplish the mission. Ready? Go!
