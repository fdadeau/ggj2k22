How to play

Meet Demonio:

Demonio has to forge souls by giving them dual personality traits:

good vs. evil
optimism vs. pessimism
romantism vs. perversity

Demonio can be moved with the keyboard (arrows or ZQSD)

Step 1. Action the source to get a virgin soul.

Actions are performed using space key.

Step 2. Place the soul in front a TV for a sufficient time, to give it one of the caracteristics.

For each soul, caracteristics can be obtained in any order.

Step 3. Wait for angels or succubus to bring tokens that power the TV with the appropriate programs...

or shoot them before they deliver undesired tokens.

"Nice shot, Demonio!"

Step 4. Deliver the soul to the exit when it is ready!

Complete the mission before the time is over to reach the next level.

NB. Souls can created, forged and delivered in any order.
