How to play

Demonio is in charge of preparing souls for their next life. His jobs is to assign them caracteristics that are dual: good/bad, optimistic/pessimistic, romantic/perverse.

Create a new "empty" soul by using the pump.

Place the soul in front a TV for a sufficient time, to give it the expected caracteristics.

TVs broadcast programs that will forge the souls' caracteristics. TV are powered by tokens which determine the kind of programs that are displayed on the screen.

Tokens are brought by little angels that can be shot by Demonio.
